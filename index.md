## Welcome to Infini-Change

Infini-Change is dedicated to empowering people to both learn about and change the systems shaping our world. Here, you’ll find a guided education program on major social topics—starting with anti-capitalism and socialism—and powerful tools and resources so you can take meaningful action for justice and equality.

Our goal: Help you go from curious and concerned, to informed and actively making change—whether that’s local, national, or global.
